Is the Green Space in Greater Sydney Distributed Fairly? Created by Eden Brender
for UNSW BENV7728
This Map explores the relationship between public open space and socioeconomic status

Each suburb has attributes for:
Public space by area percentage and per capita
Public space (including bushland) by area percentage and per capita
Socioeconoimic status - index of relative socioeconomic advantage and disadvantage (IRSAD)
 How to Use the Map

Click and drag to pan around the map
Scroll to zoom
Click on a suburb to see attributes
